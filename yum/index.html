Spinach Salad

with Preserved Lemon & Olives

  • 2 pounds fresh spinach
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ preserved lemon peel, chopped
  • ½ cup violet olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1 pinch salt and black pepper

  1. 1 Wash the spinach and remove the stems only if too thick or hard.
  2. 2 Put the leaves in a large pan covered, and set over low heat until the leaves crumble into a soft mass. They will steam in the water that clings to them in one to two minutes. Drain well.
  3. 3 Heat the garlic in one tablespoon of oil in a large pan until the aroma rises.
  4. 4 Add the chopped preserved lemon peel, chopped olives, and cooked spinach. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well and cook over high heat for a minute or two.
  5. 5 Stir in the remaining oil, garnish with the reserved olives and serve cold.
