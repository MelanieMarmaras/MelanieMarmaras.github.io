100 THINGS

infographic poster // november 2014

  • make paper
  • client information architecture II class
  • discipline info graphics, publication, typography, UX
  • position designer, production

Over time I had derived a collection of over 100 cards and letters I had received over time. This poster is a visual representation of that. I targeted attributes of reasons for keeping, the event, whom it was, the year, ratings by content and visual appearance, item type, and if it was a repeated item.
