CANclimb INTERFACE

62 mountains of new england // may 2015

  • make website
  • client information architecture II class
  • discipline info graphics, typography, branding, UX
  • position designer
  • tag team angela harrington

This is a identity system for frequent and new users to navigate through mountain climbing, specifically the 62 mountains of new england. The interface is a service for filtering through the different mountains to give an easier and quicker insight to mountain climbing. The interface narrates you through topics such as travel time, trail time, mountain activity, and mountain difficulty.
