ENERGY IN THE ENVIORMENT

10 things booklet // october 2014

  • make paper, accordion fold
  • client graphic design II class
  • discipline publication, typography, photography
  • position designer, photographer, production

After taking a trip to Haystack over in Main I was immediately drawn into the environment and how much it can influence our world. That later became the inspiration for my 10 things to know about energy booklet. I go over topics of how much the environment is used to harness or exude energy. Topics include the different energy types (kinetic, chemical, thermal, etc.) and ways on conserving and utilizing energy.
