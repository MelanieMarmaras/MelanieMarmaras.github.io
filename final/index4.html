This is a personal portfolio of Melanie Ann Marmaras with works completed as a student and designer. Works include, branding, publication, user experience, packaging, and typography. Interests focus in user experience research and user experience design. When not designing I can be found being involved in high energy activities such as mountain climbing, dancing, and kayaking. Although—sometimes I do enjoy sitting in a broadway theater of the latest musical. Whatever it is you find me doing I’m doing it with full zest and energy!
