CANclimb PAMPHLET

62 mountains of new england // may 2015

  • make paper, booklet fold
  • client information architecture II class
  • discipline info graphics, typography, branding, UX
  • position designer, production
  • tag team angela harrington

This is a identity system for frequent and new users to navigate through mountain climbing, specifically the 62 mountains of new england. The pamphlet acts as a tool to introduce the user to the world of mountain climbing while breaking the misinterpretations of the hype of the mountain climbing world. The pamphlet narrates you through topics such as gear, seasonal frequency, hiking time on average, location, name, and difficulty. As a special feature it opens up into a post the engage and encourage the user to complete the experience.
