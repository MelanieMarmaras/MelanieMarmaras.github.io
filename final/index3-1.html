SPIRITUAL VISION

type expressions book // march 2015

  • make paper, laser cut, accordion fold
  • client typography III class
  • discipline publication, typography, UX
  • position designer, production

A typographic expressions book telling the story of Hellen Keller's view on top the Empire State building when first built in 1931. She guides the user with her words through her imagination and encourages mankind to think deeper and open up their own spiritual vision by using ones imagination and not accept the reality that which they see with physical eyes.
