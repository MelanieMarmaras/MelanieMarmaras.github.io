ESTEEM GALLERY

logo design and process // november 2014

  • make paper, saddle stitch
  • client graphic design II class
  • discipline publication
  • position designer, production

As a hypothetical gallery the esteem gallery is for artists who try to make a living with their work. The gallery is located in Back Bay of Boston, MA as to introduce a different social class to their work in order to gain higher selling points. I took the idea of two different social classes interacting and linking with one another as my inspiration for the logo design.
